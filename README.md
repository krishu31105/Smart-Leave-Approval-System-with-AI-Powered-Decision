# Smart-Leave-Approval-System-with-AI-Powered-Decision

## Overview

The Smart Leave Approval System is a custom ServiceNow application designed to automate and simplify the employee leave management process. The system enables employees to submit leave requests, managers to review and approve or reject requests, and administrators to monitor leave activities through dashboards and reports.

The application incorporates AI-powered decision support by automatically assessing leave request risk levels and providing recommendations to assist managers during the approval process.

---

## Features

### Employee Features

* Apply for leave requests.
* View leave request history.
* Track approval status.
* Submit leave reasons and details.

### Manager Features

* Review leave requests.
* Approve or reject requests.
* View AI-generated risk assessments.

### AI-Powered Decision Support

* Automatic leave duration calculation.
* AI Risk Assessment (Low, Medium, High).

## Workflow

Employee Submits Leave Request
↓
Calculate Total Days
↓
Generate AI Risk Assessment
↓
Generate AI Recommendation
↓
Set Status = Pending
↓
Manager Approval
↓
Approved / Rejected

---

## AI Risk Assessment Logic

| Leave Duration   | Risk Level  |
| ---------------- | ----------- |
| 1 – 3 Days       | Low Risk    |
| 4 – 7 Days       | Medium Risk |
| More than 7 Days | High Risk   |
